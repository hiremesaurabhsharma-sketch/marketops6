Media Buying FAQs

How does MarketOps deal with iOS cookie protection and cookie blocking?

We implement fully server-side conversions APIs (CAPI) on AWS and secure third-party attribution models. This completely bypasses standard browser-level blocks, providing ad trackers with clear, secure data points for optimal biddings.

What is our average ad testing velocity each week?

For active ad accounts, we design and launch 15 to 25 distinct creative variations weekly. This covers minor hook variations, layout swaps, music sync adjustments, and copy tests to completely block visual fatigue.

Do we have direct access to our advertising accounts?

Yes, 100%. You retain full administrative ownership and direct billing access over every platform account. We operate strictly as partners under clear, authorized roles inside your channels.
